Cauliflower Korma

The creamy Indian dish perfect for a vegan dinner.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

  • Sunflower oil 1 tbsp
  • Onion 1
  • Cauliflower 1 bunch
  • Garlic 1 clove
  • Tumeric ground 1 tsp
  • Cumin ground 1 tsp
  • Curry paste mild 1 tbsp
  • Coconut milk 1 400ml can
  • Lentils green 1 400ml can
  • Sultanas 100g
  • Lemon 1 whole
  • Coconut yogurt 2 tbsp
  • Coriander 50g

How to make!

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan, add the onion and cauliflower and cook over a medium-high heat for 3-4 minutes, stirring frequently to avoid sticking. Stir in the garlic, turmeric, cumin and curry paste and cook for further minute.
  2. Pour in the coconut milk and green lentils, then loosely place a lid over the pan. Cook for 20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from the heat and stir through the sultanas. Squeeze in the lemon juice and season to taste with sea salt. Stiur through the coconut yogurt, then scatter over the coriander before serving.

Tip

Sultanas add a pop of sweetness to the korma, but if you don't want to use them, or if your store cupboard is depleted, replace with a generous tablespoon of mango chutney.
