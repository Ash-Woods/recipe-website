Almond Butter Smoothie

This healthy smoothie recipe is packed with protein, fiber, unsaturated fats, and essential vitamins and minerals.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

  • Banana
  • Mangos 1/2 cup
  • Strawberries 1/2 cup
  • Plain Greek yogurt 1/4 cup
  • Almond butter 1 tbsp
  • Baby spinach 1/4 cup
  • Unsweetened oat milk 1/2 cup
  • Ginger 10g

How to make!

  1. Place all ingredients in a blender; process until smooth.
