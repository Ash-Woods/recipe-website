Warm Panzanella

A wonderful dish with rustic bread and tomato salad, gently oven-baked ingredients with softened flavours.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

  • Tomato cherry 300g
  • Onion red 1/2 whole
  • Capsicum red 4
  • Sourdough
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp
  • Parsley 50g
  • Sea salt 1/2 tsp

How to make!

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200c.
  2. Arrange the tomatoes, red onion, capsicum, and bread in an ovenproof dish,then bake inthe oven for 15 minutes.
  3. Remove from the oven and drizzle with extra olive oil. Season with salt and pepper, toss, then scatter with parsley.

Tip

Remove bread slices that are mostly destined for the bin in this warming salad.
