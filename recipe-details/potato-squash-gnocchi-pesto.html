Potato & Squash Gnocchi Pesto

A traditional Italian dish that serves as a great vegan alternative with butternut pumpkin and pesto trapanese.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

Gnocchi

  • Potato 750g
  • Butternut Squash 500g
  • Olive oil 2 tbsp
  • Plain flour 1 cup

Pesto Trapanese

  • Tomato 3
  • Garlic clove 4
  • Basil 50g
  • Almonds blanced 1 cup
  • Olive oil 2/3 cup

How to make!

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 200c.
  2. First, make the pesto. Place all the ingredients in a food processor and blend until finely chopped. Alternatively, finely chop all the ingredients by hand, and stir everything together to give a chunk sauce. Season and set aside.
  3. To make the gnocci, price the potatoes two or three times with a fork ad bake in oven for about 35 minutes to 1 hour, or until tender.
  4. Place the butternut squash cubes on a baking track, add the olive oil and a pinch of salt and toss well. Add to the oven and roast for 30-40minutes or until very tender.
  5. When the potatoes are cool enough to handle, but still warm, peel off the skins, drop into a large bowl and mash well until smooth. Add the cooked squash to the bowl with the potato and mash well once more.
  6. Beat in the flour a little at a time. Stop adding flour when the mixture is smooth and slightly sticky. Season with salt.
  7. Turn the mixture out onto a floured board and roll out into cylinders about 34cm long with a 2cm diameter. Cut each roll into 3cm lengths.
  8. Taking one piece at a time, press the gnocchi onto a floured fork. Roll each piece slightly while pressing it along the pronts and flip it off the fork onto a floured plate.
  9. Bring a large pan of water to the boil and rop in 20-25 pieces of gnocchi at a time. They will rise to the surfaec very quickly. Let them cook for 10-15seconds more. lift them out with a slotted spoon and keep them warm while you continue with the remaining gnocchi.
