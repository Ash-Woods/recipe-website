Hashbrowns with Tumeric Cashew Sauce

A great combination of has brown, sweet potato pumpkinand creamy cashew sauce,all piled high with herbs.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

Main Dish

  • Potatoes 350g
  • Plain flour 2 tbsp
  • Rosemary chopped 2 tbsp
  • Avocado oil 1 tbsp

Pumpkin and Chickpea Topping

  • Pumpkin 150g
  • Chickpeas 1/2 cup
  • Maple Syrup 2 tsp
  • Olive Oil 2 tsp

Tumeric-Cashew Sauce

  • Cashews 50g
  • Tumeric ground 1/4
  • Lemon 1 whole
  • Olive oil 1 tsp
  • Apple cider vinegar 1 tsp

To Serve

  • Avocado
  • Dill 50g
  • Hemp seeds 1 tbsp

How to make!

  1. Pre-heat oven to 180c fan-forced.
  2. Combine the ingredients for the pumpkin and chickpea topping in a baking dish and bake for 35minutes or until golden and crsip. Set aside.
  3. 'Meanwhile', grate the potatoes into a colander. Rinse with water, then squeeze out as much liquid as possible. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towel or a clean tea towel to completely dry.
  4. To make the turmeric-cashew sauce, place all the ingredients and 3 tablespoons of water in a blender and blend until smooth. Check the seasoning and add mroe salt, if desired. Set aside.
  5. Transfer the potato to a large bowl and stir through the flour, herbs and salt.
  6. Heat the oil in large frying pan over high heat and spoon in 1/3 cup of the potato mixture to make each hashbrown.
  7. Spread each hashbrown out to firm a 1cm -thick circle. Fry on each side for 2-3 minutes or until golden brown and crisp. Then transfer to a plate lined with paper towel to soeak up excess oil.
  8. Place a hashbrown on each plate and top with the pumpkin and chickpea mixture. Position the remaining hashbrowns on top and spoon the turmeric-cashew sauce around the plate.

Tip

Make sure you check on the items in the oven while cooking the potatoes, otherwise they may burn quickly.
