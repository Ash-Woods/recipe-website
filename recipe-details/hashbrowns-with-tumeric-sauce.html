Hashbrowns with Tumeric Cashew Sauce

A great combination of has brown, sweet potato pumpkinand creamy cashew sauce,all piled high with herbs.

Ingredients

Below is a list of required ingredients:

  • 1 cup almond milk (or more if needed to blend)
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 cup spinach (not packed down)
  • 2 bananas, frozen
  • 1 cup mango (fresh or frozen)

How to make!

Place all ingredients in a blender; process until smooth.
